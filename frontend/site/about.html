Athokpam Dinesh Singh

Academic Counsellor | Career Guidance Expert

Athokpam Dinesh Singh is a highly experienced Academic Counsellor with over 13+ years of expertise in student guidance, career counselling, and academic advisory.

He holds an MBA in Marketing & Finance (2012) and has worked with reputed institutions across the Northeast. He began his professional journey with Triumphant Institute of Management Education (T.I.M.E.), Guwahati, where he was actively involved in delivering student training under a North Eastern Council (NEC) sponsored programme.

He further served at The Assam Royal Global University, Guwahati for 10 years as a Counsellor-cum-Manager, leading counselling operations and managing student outreach for the entire Manipur region.

With deep industry insight and a student-centric approach, he specializes in:

  • Career Counselling & Academic Planning
  • MBA & Higher Education Guidance
  • Student Mentorship & Skill Development
  • Admission Strategy & Decision Support

He is committed to empowering students with the right direction, helping them make informed career choices and achieve long-term success.
